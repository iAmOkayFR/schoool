Bal Vikas School, Model Town, Panipat, was established in 1985 by Shri Rajeshwar Nath under the aegis of the Raj Charitable Trust as part of its commitment to social service. Over the years, the school has earned a distinguished reputation in the state for delivering quality education at an affordable fee. As a Senior Secondary School, it offers three academic streams at the +2 level while maintaining an optimal student-teacher ratio of 1:20.

Our school strongly believes in the all-round development of students, offering a wide range of extracurricular activities that go beyond academics. To promote physical fitness and sportsmanship, students can participate in activities such as aerobics, kho-kho, badminton, table tennis, basketball, volleyball, and judo/taekwondo. These sports help build teamwork, discipline, and endurance.

For students with a passion for performing arts, we offer vocal and instrumental music, dance, dramatics in both English and Hindi, and creative writing to encourage self-expression and creativity. Budding scientists and environmentalists can join the Science & Eco Club, while those interested in technology can explore advanced learning through computer-based activities.

Additionally, students can engage in home science & drawing, heritage education, and yoga, fostering life skills, cultural awareness, and mindfulness. These activities ensure that students develop holistically, nurturing their talents, confidence, and leadership abilities alongside their academic growth
