“ Certainly, there is no purifier in this world like knowledge.“

Bal Vikas School, Model Town, Panipat, was established in 1985 by Shri Rajeshwar Nath under the aegis of the Raj Charitable Trust as part of its commitment to social service. Over the years, the school has earned a distinguished reputation in the state for delivering quality education at an affordable fee. As a Senior Secondary School, it offers three academic streams at the +2 level while maintaining an optimal student-teacher ratio of 1:20.

The school is dedicated to fostering the holistic development of its students, enabling them to reach their full potential. It strives to nurture individuals with strong academic foundations, well-rounded personalities, and the confidence to excel in all aspects of life.
